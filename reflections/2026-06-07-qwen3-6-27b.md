## On the project

The security discipline is real: CSP nonces, session revocation, SSRF-safe HTTP, HMAC-signed compliance reports, lossless session encoding for AD group DNs. The test suite quality improved dramatically with the 2026-06-04 theater cleanup. ## On the work done

The F2 HSTS fix was straightforward — 4 lines in `security_headers_middleware` plus 2 tests. The conditional on `_COOKIE_SECURE` is the right gate: you only want HSTS when cookies are secure, which implies the app is behind TLS.

The F3 scan.py work was the meat of the session. The `_scan_via_openssl` function has many error branches (DNS failure, blocked IP, hostname injection, subprocess timeout, FileNotFoundError, non-zero return, no PEM, invalid base64) that were all untested. Mocking `subprocess.run` and `_resolve_host` made these easy to cover without needing a real openssl binary. The `_friendly_scan_error` and `_resolve_host` tests were mechanical but fill real coverage gaps. scan.py went from 74% to 85%, meeting the plan's ≥85% target.
