## Problem

scan.py persists tls_verified from the verify= input parameter (lines 546, 590, 638). For the openssl path it's hardcoded False regardless of actual TLS handshake outcome. The field surfaces in the UI and scan_posture table. Rename to verify_requested or split into tls_verified (observed chain trust) and verify_requested (operator config).

Related: BC-064 (resolved) covered the broader verification-off-by-default issue.
