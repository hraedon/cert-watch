**Resolved 2026-06-02 (ChatGPT review #6).** `check_rate_limit` silently fell back
to per-process in-memory counters on any DB error, logged at DEBUG. Raised to
WARNING so the degradation is visible. (cert-watch is single-process by design, so
the multi-replica bypass angle is limited, but the silent degrade was the real nit.)
