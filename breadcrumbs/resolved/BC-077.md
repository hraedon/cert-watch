**Resolved 2026-06-02 (ChatGPT review #2).** `_scan_via_openssl` captured stderr
(`capture_output=True`) but never surfaced it, making scan failures undebuggable.
Now logs `proc.stderr` (truncated) at DEBUG on non-zero exit. No injection risk
existed (list-form subprocess); this is purely the debuggability sub-point.
