---
identifier: BC-082
title: OAuth client secret + LDAP bind password stored plaintext in kv_store
kind: security
status: open
severity: medium
---

**ChatGPT review #7.** When configured via the GUI (Plan 019), `oauth_client_secret`
and `ldap_bind_password` are persisted to the SQLite `kv_store` in plaintext (file
is 0600, owned by the service user). Env/`*_FILE` secrets avoid this; kv_store values
don't. Already acknowledged in Plan 019's risk notes.

**Fix (deferred):** at-rest encryption for sensitive kv_store values (OS keyring,
SQLCipher, or an app-level key derived from a deployment secret), or steer operators
to `*_FILE`/env for secrets and keep kv_store for non-sensitive config.
