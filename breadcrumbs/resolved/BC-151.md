## Problem

The Discover page reconciled CT coverage but did not detect mis-issued certificates or show per-issuer first-seen dates.

## Resolution

Implemented real CT mis-issuance detection and first-seen capture in `ct_monitor.py`:
- `ct_reconciliation()` now compares CT log entries against the scanned certificate by issuer name and serial number
- Mismatches are flagged as "mis-issued" and displayed in the Discover page
- `first_seen_by_issuer` is populated during the reconciliation cache warm and displayed per issuer
- Added `ct_issuer` and `ct_serial` columns to the reconciliation cache (migration 0018)
- The Discover template now shows a "Mis-issued Certificates" table and "First Seen by Issuer" table
