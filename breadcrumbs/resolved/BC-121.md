## Problem

The compliance report showed CAA as "Not collected" because CAA lookup was an on-demand endpoint, not a scan field. The CAA metric was always N/A in the compliance report.

## Resolution

Added `caa_present` (INTEGER) and `caa_records` (TEXT) columns to `scan_posture` (migration 0017). `scan_host()` now performs a CAA DNS lookup during the scan and stores the result:
- `caa_present=True` when CAA records exist
- `caa_present=False` when no CAA records exist
- `caa_present=None` when lookup fails (e.g., DNS error)

`evaluate_posture()` incorporates CAA presence into posture grading (bonus for present, penalty for absent). `build_compliance_report()` computes the CAA percentage from stored posture rows. The compliance report now shows a real CAA adoption metric instead of "Not collected".
