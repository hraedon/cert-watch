# BC-039: Empty-state markup is inconsistent across templates

**Type:** todo
**Severity:** low
**Status:** new
**Filed:** 2026-05-29

## Problem

Empty-state rendering uses two different patterns depending on the template:

- **dashboard.html**: `.cw-empty` component *inside* a `.cw-panel`
- **scan_history.html** / **alerts.html**: `.cw-empty-state` applied directly to the `.cw-panel`

This means fixes to empty-state padding, icon size, or background must be tested on multiple pages and risk regressing one pattern while fixing another.

## Suggested fix

Standardize on one pattern: either a dedicated `.cw-empty` component that is dropped into any panel, or a `.cw-panel.cw-empty-state` modifier. Update all three templates and document the canonical pattern in AGENTS.md or a component reference.
