## Problem

`routes/hosts.py` has its own `_ssrf_check_host()` that duplicates logic from `scan.py` and `http_client.py`. The two paths can diverge.

## Suggested fix

Centralize SSRF pre-check logic in a single module (`http_client.py` or `scan.py`) and have both paths call it.
