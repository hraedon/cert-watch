## Problem

The Discover page (`routes/views.py`) uses hardcoded issuer name fragments (`NOT LIKE '%Let%'`, `NOT LIKE '%ISRG%'`, etc.) to identify private-CA hosts. This is brittle and will miss private CAs with unexpected names.

## Suggested fix

Replace hardcoded issuer fragments with a configurable allowlist or use the trust anchor table to determine private vs. public CA.
