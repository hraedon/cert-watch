Comma-separated CIDR ranges that cert-watch may scan. Leave blank to allow all private addresses (RFC 1918), or list only the internal networks you intend to monitor — anything outside them (and outside public hosts) is refused. Loopback and link-local (incl. cloud metadata) are always blocked. You can change this later under Settings or via the CERT_WATCH_ALLOWED_SUBNETS environment variable.
You can also configure authentication via environment variables (AUTH_PROVIDER, CERT_WATCH_LOCAL_ADMIN_USER, CERT_WATCH_LOCAL_ADMIN_PASSWORD_HASH). See the runbook for details.
